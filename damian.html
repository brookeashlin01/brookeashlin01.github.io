Description

Damian is a 4 year old male Flame Point Siamese. The nature of being a Flame Point Siamese means his body mostly consists of a lovely cream color. His face--starting from his nose and moving in an outward direction up his forehead--has a burst of apricot colored fur, which has traveled up to his ears, which are also apricot in color. Along with his little paws and his tail, which surprisingly has little rings of cream and apricot dispersed along the tail, giving him the appearance of what some say is "similar to that of a racoon tail". Various gradient patches--of the same previously mentioned apricot colored fur--is sprawled out across his body.

Origin Stories

Damian

Damian was origianlly a feral kitten from a local feral colony in Gilford, NH. He was trapped at ~4 months old, and by the time I adopted him, he was ~6 months old. I brought him home and it took weeks for him to get used to being in a home and to get used to me, but eventually he bonded with me and enjoyed getting attention and recieving & giving affection. I call him my 'reformed feral kitty', since he is no longer truly feral. Damian's original name from the shelter was Hamilton, but I didn't like the name so I looked up Pokemon-related names and chose the name Damian because he was the original owner of Ash Ketchum's Charmander--I though of naming him charmander, because he was orange, but decided against the silly name and chose one loosely related to the color orange instead.
