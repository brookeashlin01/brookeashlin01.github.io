Description

Ash is an almost 5 year old male Domestic Short Hair with a mostly black body and a white nose bridge, mouth, neck, chest, and belly. These markings are commonly referred to as a "tuxedo" color pattern. He also has what are adoringly called "socks"--white markings that start at least halfway down his leg, all the way to the tip of his paw. The rest of him--his tail included--is solid black.

Origin Story

Ash was originally an ex's sister-in-law's (Kat) kitten from her cat's (Kota) most recent litter in January 2017. Ash was about 4 months old when I took him into my care. Kat and her daughter were living with my ex's grandparent's in their apartment and they were going to take him to the local NH Humane Society within the next week. My ex and I were looking for a kitten at the time and offered to take him. They all agreed that my ex and I could take Ash. His full name is Ash Ketchum, and was named by my ex's niece since he was technically her kitten. I appreciated the Pokemon reference and kept the name Ash Ketchum, calling him Ash for short.
